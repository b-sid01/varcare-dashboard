Varcare Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What we collect

When you book an appointment through Varcare, we collect your name, phone number, age, and appointment time. This information is necessary to manage your appointment with your doctor.

How we use your data

Your data is used solely to manage your appointments and communicate with you about your healthcare. We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

Who we share it with

Your data is shared only with the doctor whose clinic you are booking with. We do not sell or share your data with any third parties.

How we store it

Your data is stored securely on encrypted servers. We follow industry standard security practices to protect your information.

Your rights

You have the right to request deletion of your data at any time by replying DELETE to our WhatsApp number. You also have the right to access your data by contacting us.

Prescription disclaimer

Varcare is a clinic management tool. Prescriptions generated through Varcare are valid only for in-person consultations with a registered medical practitioner. This platform does not facilitate telemedicine consultations.
